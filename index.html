Frequently Asked Questions

How to Flip Items in OSRS?

Flipping in Old School RuneScape (OSRS) involves buying items low on the Grand Exchange and selling high for profit. Here are some tips:

  • Start with high-volume items like runes, ores, or gems to ensure quick buys/sells.
  • Use the search bar in Grant Exchange to check real-time prices and spreads—aim for items with at least a 50k GP profit goal per flip.
  • Buy at "Smart Low Buy" prices and sell at "Smart High Sell" suggestions to account for margins and taxes (2% GE tax, capped at 5m GP).
  • Respect buy limits (e.g., 10k for bars)—use the tool's buy limit adjuster to calculate capital needed.
  • Monitor trends with the day/week/month charts; flip during peak player times for faster fills.
  • Avoid over-investing—start small in F2P mode with items like steel bars, then scale to Members exclusives like potions.

What's the Difference Between F2P and Members Mode?

F2P mode filters out Members-only items for free-to-play users, while Members mode shows everything, including high-profit items like herbs and potions. Switch via the pill buttons for tailored suggestions.

How Accurate Are the Profit Calculations?

Profits are estimates based on current GE data, including 2% tax. Actual results vary with market fluctuations—use the watchlist and refresh button for live updates.
